1. Information We Collect

We collect ZERO personal information from your posture tracking.

NeckGuard operates entirely on your device. The app uses:

  • Motion Sensor Data: Real-time data from your AirPods' motion sensors to track head position (processed locally, never transmitted)
  • Camera/Face Tracking: TrueDepth camera for enhanced head position tracking (processed in real-time, images are never saved or transmitted)
  • Authentication Data: When you sign in with Google, we only receive your email address and name (handled securely by Google/Firebase)

2. How We Use Information

All posture tracking data:

  • Is processed locally on your iPhone
  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers (we don't even have servers)
  • Is stored only in your iPhone's secure local storage
  • Is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

3. Third-Party Services

NeckGuard uses the following third-party services for authentication only:

Google Sign-In

  • Used exclusively for secure authentication
  • Governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  • We receive only your email address and name
  • No posture tracking data is shared with Google
  • No additional personal data is collected or shared

Firebase Authentication

  • Stores only authentication credentials securely
  • Governed by Firebase Privacy Policy
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are enabled
  • No advertising or data collection features are used

4. Data Storage

NeckGuard stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Your posture tracking preferences and customized thresholds
  • Session history and posture quality statistics
  • Calibration data for accurate head tracking
  • Authentication tokens (stored securely in iOS Keychain)
  • App settings and user preferences

Important: All this data is automatically and permanently deleted when you uninstall the app. We cannot recover it because it never leaves your device.

5. Data Retention

NeckGuard follows a strict local-only data retention policy:

  • Authentication Tokens: Stored locally in iOS Keychain and persist until you sign out or delete the app
  • User Session Data: Maintained in secure local storage until you sign out
  • Posture Tracking Data: All angles, session times, and statistics are stored locally in your device's app storage and never transmitted to servers
  • App Settings: Saved locally in UserDefaults and persist until app deletion
  • Permanent Deletion: All data is permanently and irreversibly deleted when you uninstall the app from your device

6. Camera and Motion Sensor Usage

Camera

  • Used only for real-time face and head position tracking
  • Camera frames are processed instantly on-device using ARKit
  • Images are never saved, stored, or transmitted
  • Processing happens in real-time and frames are immediately discarded
  • You can revoke camera access anytime in iOS Settings

Motion Sensors

  • AirPods motion sensor data is used for precise head tilt detection
  • Data is processed in real-time for immediate posture feedback
  • Motion data is not stored or logged
  • Used only while tracking is active

Face Tracking

  • TrueDepth camera used for enhanced head position accuracy
  • Face mesh data processed locally, never transmitted
  • No facial recognition or identity detection is performed
  • Used solely for measuring head orientation angles

No Cross-App Tracking: NeckGuard does NOT track your activity across other companies' apps or websites. We do not collect or share data for advertising, marketing, or behavioral tracking purposes.

Privacy commitments:

  • We do not request the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission
  • We do not use tracking identifiers (IDFA, etc.)
  • We do not share data with advertising networks or data brokers
  • All tracking and privacy settings in iOS remain under your complete control
  • Your usage of NeckGuard is not monitored or tracked for any purpose

8. Analytics & Third-Party SDKs

Zero Analytics Tracking: NeckGuard does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools of any kind.

Technical details:

  • Firebase Analytics: Explicitly disabled in our implementation
  • Google Sign-In: Used only for authentication, not for tracking or analytics
  • No Crash Reporting: We do not use third-party crash reporting services
  • No Behavior Tracking: Your in-app behavior is not monitored or logged
  • No Attribution Tracking: We do not track where you downloaded the app from

9. Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or transmit your data to anyone. Ever.

Your posture tracking data:

  • Stays on your device
  • Is never uploaded to any server
  • Is never shared with third parties
  • Is never used for advertising or analytics

11. Your Privacy Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally on your device in the app's storage
  • Deletion: Sign out or uninstall the app to permanently delete all local data
  • Portability: Your data stays on your device and can be accessed anytime
  • Revoke Permissions: Disable camera/motion access in iOS Settings
  • Account Deletion: Sign out to remove authentication data

12. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • All network communications use secure HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Authentication credentials stored in iOS Keychain (hardware-encrypted)
  • No sensitive data is transmitted over the network
  • App follows Apple's security best practices
  • Regular security updates and patches

13. International Data Transfers

Since all posture tracking data stays on your device, there are no international data transfers. Authentication data handled by Google/Firebase follows their respective data transfer policies.
