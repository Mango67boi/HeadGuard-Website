Frequently Asked Questions

Which AirPods are supported?

NeckGuard works with AirPods Pro (1st gen or later) and AirPods Max. These models have the motion sensors required for precise head tracking.

How accurate is the tracking?

Very accurate when properly calibrated. The app uses advanced motion sensors in your AirPods combined with optional camera-based face tracking for maximum precision. Make sure your AirPods fit snugly for best results.

Does it work without AirPods?

The app can run, but posture tracking requires AirPods Pro or AirPods Max with motion sensors. These are essential for head position detection.

Does this drain my battery?

NeckGuard is optimized for minimal battery impact. CoreMotion and camera tracking are both very energy-efficient on iOS.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All your posture data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I use this while working?

Yes. NeckGuard is perfect for desk work, studying, or any activity where you're using your computer or phone. It helps you maintain good posture throughout your day.

How do I calibrate the app?

Simply sit in your ideal posture position and start tracking. The app automatically calibrates to your natural posture within the first few seconds.

Troubleshooting

App says "AirPods not connected"

Make sure your AirPods are properly connected to your iPhone and are in your ears. Check Bluetooth settings to verify the connection.

Tracking seems inaccurate

Try recalibrating by tapping the recalibrate button in the app. Ensure your AirPods fit properly and aren't loose.

Google Sign-In isn't working

Make sure you have a stable internet connection. If issues persist, try signing out and back in again.

System Requirements

  • iOS 16.0 or later
  • AirPods Pro (1st gen or later) or AirPods Max
  • iPhone with TrueDepth camera (recommended for enhanced tracking)
  • Active internet connection for authentication

Quick Start Guide

  1. Connect your AirPods Pro or AirPods Max
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Grant necessary permissions (camera, motion sensors)
  4. Sit in your ideal posture position
  5. Tap "Start Tracking"
  6. The app calibrates automatically
  7. Receive gentle alerts when your posture needs adjustment

Privacy First: All your tracking data stays on your device. We never collect, store, or share your personal information.
